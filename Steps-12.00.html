Add Age Groups 12

In this step we'll group ages into four groups. Children (6-14), Youth (15-24), Adults (25-64), Seniors 65 plus. 

Steps: (Age Group)

  1. in the Fields pane expand Shooting table, then right click on Age column. 
  2. select New Group, a group window opens. 
  3. Name the new group 'Age Group', then change the Group type to List 
  4. in the Ungrouped values select 6 hold shift and click 14 then click on Group or press Enter 
  5. the group appears in the right pane under Groups and members, 
  6. double click the group and rename it to Children. 
  7. follow the same steps to group Youth, Adults, and Seniors 

if you run into any issues, make sure to inspect and or replace bad values in the 'Age' column in Power Query.
