Step: Adding Total Shooting and Shooting Per Dar KPI cards

  1. Make sure the nothing is select in the canvas, then in the Fields pane expand the 'Measures Folder'
  2. Under the Visualizations pane select the  Dynamic KPI Card by Sereviso, you can get for free from Power BI market by click the  ellipsis and search for it. 
  3. once you get it click on it to add it to the canvas, then drag and drop or click the tick box next to the Total Shooting  measure to add it to the card. 
  4. the format it as follow:

  5. Format: 

    1. Select the card, under Visualizations pane click  and change the KPI Name to Total Shooting and make the fond-size = 16
    2. Under Indicator make the color = Black, Font-size = 40 and under Comparison 1 & 2 hide the comparisons 
    3. In Frame Color change all 3 colors to White, in General setting change Height = 95, Width = 158 and change 
    4. Position Horizontal = 0, Vertical = 41, then turn both Title and Effects off


Follow above steps and add Shooting Per Day card. We'll change its colors to match our charts later on. 

All cards will have same size however, the positions will differ. We'll just place the next card right behind the first card and will align them all later once we have them all. 


Steps: Threats, %MH (Mental Health), % Flee, and % Body Cam Cards 

  1. Add another Dynamic card then select   measure to add it to the card. 
  2. Next, drag and drop the Threats YoY measure in Comparison 1 under Visualizations pane
  3. the format it as follow:
  4. Format:
    1. Select the card, under Visualizations click  and change the KPI Name to Total Shooting and make the fond size 16
    2. Under Indicator make the color = Black, Font-size = 22 
    3. Turn/Show Comparison 1 but turn of 2, make the Font-size =16, and Prefix = YoY and make Value Decimal Places to 0
    4. In Frame Color change all 3 colors to White, in General setting change Height = 95, Width = 158 and change 
    5. Position place it next to the last card, Vertical = 41, then turn both Title and Effects off

Follow these steps and add the remaining 3 cards. 


Steps: Adding Time Slicer

  1. Click on the Slicer visual 
  2. Drag the Year column from Date table to the slicer 
  3. Under Slicer Settings change the Style to Between 
  4. Change the Font-size to 15, Color to Black, and Background to None
  5. Turn Slicer header off, 
  6. In the Property setting change Height to 96 and Width to 324 and make the position to Horizontal to 956 and Vertical to 41
  7. Turn Title and Effects off
