Steps: adding Waterfall Chart 

  1. Select the visual, from the ribbon click Copy then Paste to duplicate the first chart
  2. Drag the copy and align to the right of the first chart, with the copy visual selected click on the Waterfall chart in the Visualizations pane. 
  3. From the Fields pane expand the Category table, drag the Category column in the Category field 
  4. Drag the Category Total measure in the Y-axis field, finally drag all 6 KPIs in the Tooltips field. 

  5. Format:

    1. under Format change the colors like so:
    2. Increase = Red
    3. Decrease = Green
