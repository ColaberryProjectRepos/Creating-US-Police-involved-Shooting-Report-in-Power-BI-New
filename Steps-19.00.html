Step: Add Map Visual 

  1. Duplicate the Age chart, change the visual into a Map  chart from the Visualization pane. 
  2. Drag State and City in the Location field, and drag Total Shooting in the Bubble size. 
  3.  Format: 
    1. In the Visual under Formatting change the Bubbles size to 10px, change the Width to 481 in the Properties and drag the chart far most right and align bottom with the other 2 bottom charts.
