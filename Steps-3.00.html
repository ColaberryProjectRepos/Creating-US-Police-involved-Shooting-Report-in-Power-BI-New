Executive Summary:



Issues Encountered:


1. The challenge faced is transforming a data source into useful information for analysis. This requires the cleaning and preparation of the data, including removing any irrelevant or missing information, and converting non-numeric values into a numerical format for further analysis. The ultimate goal is to create clean and usable data for creating measures and columns for data visualization.

2. The creation of the dashboard was hindered by a shortage of adequate data for measure creation. Essential information, such as a Date table and columns for Year, Month, Week, and Day, were missing from the original data source, requiring additional time and effort to gather and organize the necessary information. This resulted in a need to create additional data to ensure accurate and informative measures for the dashboard.

3. The final challenge in creating the dashboard was selecting effective visualizations to communicate the data insights and key takeaways to viewers. This required not only choosing the appropriate chart types but also formatting and arranging them to clearly convey the information and tell a meaningful story. To ensure the dashboard was visually appealing, easy to navigate, and highlighted important issues and progress, careful consideration was given to the design and layout of the dashboard. The goal was to create a dashboard that effectively communicates the data and insights in a clear and impactful manner.
